MIT Campus, Boston - Nov 2022 üåü

In November 2022, my friend graciously invited me to explore the MIT campus in Boston, and the experience was nothing short of incredible. The journey from Chicago to Boston was an adventure in itself, filled with anticipation. The highlight of our visit was stepping into the Kresge Auditorium, a colossal space boasting a 200-seat theater and a 1,200-seat performance hall. Watching a movie there was a cinematic experience like no other.

Our exploration continued as we wandered through the campus, venturing into the library and various departments that showcased MIT's academic prowess. One standout was the Borderline Mural Project, adorning a 200-foot wall in the tunnels beneath the campus. These tunnels, often used during inclement weather, were transformed into an artistic haven. I couldn't resist capturing the magic on video, which you can catch above.

MIT's blend of innovation, art, and academic excellence left an indelible mark on our journey, making it a truly unforgettable experience.
